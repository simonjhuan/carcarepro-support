Privacy Policy

CarCare Pro — Last updated July 14, 2026

This policy explains what information CarCare Pro collects and how it's used.

Data you enter

Vehicle records, service history, notes, and any photos you attach are stored locally on your device. We do not operate a server or account system, and this data is not transmitted to us.

Camera and photo library

The app requests camera and photo library access only so you can attach photos to your own records. These photos stay on your device.

Advertising

CarCare Pro shows ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising identifier) and usage data to serve and measure ads, and may use this data across other apps and services per Google's own policies. Where required (for example under GDPR/UK GDPR), you'll be shown a consent form before any personalized ads are shown, and you can change your choice at any time via the app's privacy/consent settings.

See Google's Partner Sites policy for how Google uses this data.

Children's privacy

CarCare Pro is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
